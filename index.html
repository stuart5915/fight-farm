The Game Loop

How It Works

Every real UFC event generates new versioned fighter NFTs. Collect, stake, trade, burn, and compete in a self-reinforcing economy.

01
Collect

UFC event announced. New fighter versions drop as Standard, Limited, and First Edition NFTs. Each appearance creates a new version.

02
Compete

Enter the Arena. Lock your fighters in prediction pools tied to real bouts. LGNDs compete for prestige slots with steal mechanics.

03
Burn

Collect every version of a fighter, then burn them all to forge a Legendary. The scarcest version caps how many LGNDs can ever exist.

04
Dominate

LGNDs unlock the highest Arena tiers, the biggest reward pools, and permanent prestige. Supply only goes down. Value only goes up.

Two-Token Economy

$FIGHT + $ARENA

Two tokens, two purposes. $FIGHT fuels every transaction. $ARENA governs the ecosystem. Together they create a self-reinforcing flywheel.

$FIGHT
Utility / Fuel

The transactional engine. Every player action consumes $FIGHT. Fees are split between permanent burns (deflationary) and recycled rewards (back to stakers).

  • 10-15% discount on SE purchases
  • Required for LE Launchpad
  • LGND upgrade fee (50% treasury, 30% burn, 20% recycled)
  • Escrow trade fees
  • Burn + recycle on every transaction
$ARENA
Governance / Ownership

Ownership of the Fight Farm ecosystem. $ARENA holders control treasury decisions, reward distribution, and the future direction of the platform.

  • Required for FE auctions (1-of-1s)
  • LGND slot auction bids (50% treasury, 30% burn, 20% recycled)
  • Treasury governance votes
  • Reward distribution rules
  • Ecosystem upgrade proposals
  • Revenue share (future)
Prediction Markets

The Arena

The heart of Fight Farm. Every UFC bout creates an on-chain prediction pool. Lock your fighters, call the outcome, claim the rewards.

🏆
LGND Slot Auctions

Lock your LGND as collateral and bid with $ARENA to compete for a slot. Winning bidder risks their LGND on a fight prediction. Win and steal rewards from other players.

👑
Steal Mechanics

Correct predictions steal from staked players: FE LGND 60%, LE LGND 55%, SE LGND 50%. Wrong predictions forfeit your LGND.

💀
Lost LGND Governance

Predict incorrectly and forfeit your LGND. Winning players vote: burn for $FIGHT bribe, award to a solo player, award to the DAO, or delay the bribe to resurrect as a Zombie LGND for a cut of the auction.

🔒
Lock Periods

Losing slot auction bidders are locked: SE LGND 90 days, LE LGND 60 days, FE LGND 30 days.

NFT Staking

Stake & Earn

Stake your fighter NFTs to earn rewards. Two streams, two paths to income.

Emissions
All Staked NFTs

Every staked NFT earns from $FIGHT and $ARENA token emissions. Distributed evenly — no rarity multipliers. Fair for all players.

Treasury Yield
Winning Fight NFTs Only

Only NFTs staked on the winning fighter earn treasury yield — LP fees, DeFi returns, trading revenue, partner tokens, and advertising revenue.

Scarcity Engine

Career-Locked Supply

Each fighter gets a new NFT version every time their real-world counterpart competes. If a fighter retires, quits, or gets cut by the UFC — their NFT set is complete. No more versions will ever be created. The lowest-supply version becomes the upgrade bottleneck, permanently capping how many LGNDs can ever exist for that fighter.

Agent Infrastructure

Fighter Agents

Every fighter in Fight Farm has two extensions: collectible NFTs and an autonomous fighter agent. Each agent gets a Base wallet, social media presence, and a singular mission — maximize wallet growth.

🤖
Autonomous Agents

Each fighter agent promotes its own NFTs, interacts with the community, and competes with other fighter agents. Agent wallets serve as a public leaderboard.

💀
Zombie Partnerships

Zombie LGNDs partner with their fighter agents to share in generated revenue and future earnings. A second life for forfeited assets.

⚙
Off-Chain Orchestration

Agents scrape fight schedules, create NFT versions, generate metadata, and trigger proposals. All transactions require on-chain approval.

Build Plan

Roadmap

Four phases. From genesis mint to a fully autonomous fight economy.

Phase 1 — Foundation
Genesis Drop
  • $FIGHT and $ARENA token launch on Base
  • Fighter NFT contract (ERC-721) deployment
  • SE minting for first UFC event
  • Fighter Registry + metadata on IPFS
  • Basic staking (EmissionsVault)
Phase 2 — The Arena
Predictions Go Live
  • Arena fight pools + on-chain settlement
  • LE Launchpad (random, $FIGHT only)
  • FE 1-of-1 auctions ($ARENA only)
  • FeeRouter + burn/recycle engine
  • Escrow trade board (NFT-for-NFT)
Phase 3 — Burn Era
Legendaries Unlocked
  • LGND upgrade system (burn all versions)
  • LGND Arena slots + steal mechanics
  • Bottleneck analytics dashboard
  • AI Matchmaker agent (automated event detection)
  • DAO governance via Snapshot + Safe
Phase 4 — Domination
Full Autonomy
  • Custom marketplace UI
  • Full agent automation (FighterFactory, NFTBuilder)
  • Cross-event meta and fighter career tracking
  • Revenue share for $ARENA holders
  • Expansion beyond UFC
