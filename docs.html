01

Overview

Fight Farm is a combat sports collectible economy built on Base (Ethereum L2). Every real UFC event generates new fighter NFTs. Players collect versioned fighters, compete in prediction markets, burn complete sets to forge Legendaries, and govern the ecosystem through token ownership.

Core thesis: Fight Farm is a collectible sports economy where NFTs are the assets, $FIGHT is the fuel, and $ARENA represents ownership of the arena itself.

The system creates a self-reinforcing flywheel: players buy NFTs, playing requires $FIGHT, fees burn $FIGHT and fund rewards, ecosystem value increases, $ARENA governance becomes more valuable, cycle repeats.

03

Versioning System

Every fighter receives a new version number each time they appear on a UFC card. Version 1 is their first Fight Farm appearance, incrementing by +1 per event. Versions are immutable once committed.

This creates a growing collection per fighter over their career. A fighter who has appeared at 5 UFC events has 5 versions. To forge their Legendary, you need one SE from every version.

The Bottleneck: The scarcest version of any fighter caps how many LGNDs can ever exist. When it reaches zero supply, no more Legendaries can be forged. Ever.
04

Tokens

$FIGHT — Utility / Fuel

The transactional engine of Fight Farm. Every player action consumes $FIGHT. Fees are split between permanent burns (deflationary) and recycled rewards (back to stakers).

  • 10-15% discount on SE purchases
  • Required for LE Launchpad purchases
  • LGND upgrade fee (100% burn)
  • Arena entry and predictions
  • Escrow trade fees

$ARENA — Governance / Ownership

Ownership of the Fight Farm ecosystem. $ARENA holders control treasury decisions, reward distribution rules, and the platform's future direction.

  • Required for FE auctions (1-of-1s)
  • Treasury governance via Snapshot + Safe
  • Reward distribution rules
  • Ecosystem upgrade proposals
  • Future revenue share
05

The Arena

The Arena creates on-chain prediction pools for every UFC bout. Players lock fighters, predict outcomes, and claim rewards when fights resolve.

  • Fight Pools: Tied to real bout slots (not specific fighters). Pools survive fighter replacements.
  • LGND Slots: Legendary holders compete for prestige positions with collateral locks and steal mechanics.
  • State Machine: Scheduled → Locked → Resolved → Claimable
  • 3% fee: 2% burned, 1% recycled to rewards

Pools are keyed by stable bout identifiers (e.g., UFC326_BOUT04), not fighter names. If a fighter gets replaced, the pool updates — it doesn't cancel.

06

Legendary Upgrade

The LGND upgrade is the core burn mechanic. To forge a Legendary:

  • Own one SE of every version of that fighter
  • Burn all of them in a single transaction
  • Pay a $FIGHT upgrade fee ($75-150 equivalent, 100% burned)
  • Receive an irreplaceable LGND NFT
Version Bottleneck Ratchet: Each LGND upgrade burns one SE from every version. The scarcest version hits zero first. At that point, LGND supply is permanently capped. The formula: LGND_max = min(Float_v1, Float_v2, ..., Float_vN)

This creates a deflationary spiral: early LGNDs are cheap, but as bottleneck versions get burned, remaining upgrade paths become exponentially more valuable.

08

Staking

The EmissionsVault lets players stake fighter NFTs to earn weekly rewards. Staked NFTs are held in custody by the contract.

  • Fixed weekly emissions distributed pro-rata to stakers
  • Cooldown period on unstake to prevent gaming
  • Staked NFTs cannot be traded, burned, or used in Arena simultaneously
  • ClaimRouter enables one-click claim from all sources (vault + rewards + arena)
09

Trading

Fight Farm NFTs are tradable on OpenSea, Blur, Magic Eden, and Zora automatically via ERC-721 compatibility.

Fight Farm also provides a native EscrowTrade system for direct NFT-for-NFT swaps:

  • NFT-for-NFT only (no token-for-NFT)
  • Escrow deposit with configurable expiry
  • Bundle support (trade multiple NFTs at once)
  • $FIGHT fee routed through FeeRouter

This creates a barter economy where players can swap bottleneck versions directly without needing to sell and rebuy.
