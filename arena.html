LGND Slot Auctions

Lock your LGND as collateral and bid with $ARENA to compete in the slot auction. The winning bidder risks their LGND on a fight prediction. Other players have NFTs staked on that fight to earn rewards — winning predictions steal a percentage of those rewards. Lose your prediction, and you forfeit your LGND NFT.

Lost LGND Governance

Predict the fight outcome incorrectly and you forfeit your LGND. The winning players vote to decide its fate.

1
Burn for $FIGHT Bribe
The LGND is burned permanently. $FIGHT bribe is distributed to the winning voters.
2
Award to Solo Player
The forfeited LGND is awarded to a single player from the winning side.
3
Award to DAO
The LGND is transferred to the Fight Farm DAO treasury.
4
Delay Bribe → Zombie LGND
FE and LE only. Delay the $FIGHT bribe to resurrect the LGND as a Zombie. Voters get a cut of the Zombie LGND auction.
Zombie LGNDs

When a First Edition or Limited Edition LGND's bribe is postponed, it becomes a Zombie LGND — a transformed asset with a different purpose in the Fight Farm ecosystem.

🚫
No Arena gameplay. Zombie LGNDs cannot participate in slot auctions or make fight predictions.
🤖
Fighter agent partnerships. Zombie LGNDs partner with fighter agents to generate revenue and earn ROI.
💰
Revenue split. Players split the original bribe plus a percentage of the auctioned Zombie LGND's generated revenue.
⏰
One-year lifecycle. The Zombie LGND operates for one year from the date the bribe was postponed.
Staking in the Arena

All NFTs can be staked on Arena fights. Staking earns token emissions regardless of fight outcome, but only stakers on the winning fighter earn a share of the treasury yield.

All Stakers Earn Emissions
Every staked NFT earns $FIGHT and $ARENA token emissions. No rarity multipliers — all NFTs earn equally.
$FIGHT $ARENA
Winning Side Earns Treasury Yield
Only NFTs staked on the winning fighter receive a share of the treasury yield. Pick the wrong fighter and you miss out on yield — but you still keep your NFT and emissions.
💡
Staking is lower risk than LGND slots. Stakers never lose their NFTs. However, a winning LGND slot prediction will steal a portion of the staker rewards on that fight.
